Product Trio Agent Framework

A system designed to move from a high-entropy "Mind" to an immutable "Bedrock" of truth so coding agents build exactly what was intended. A structured Product discovery methodology running inside Claude.

This framework is designed to prevent "Semantic Noise" and "Middle Degradation"—the phenomenon where AI loses high-stakes strategic pivots in the middle of a chat. By using three distinct AI personas (Product, Technical, Quality), we ensure that every decision is vetted and anchored into persistent artifacts before engineering begins.

It's not just about speed; it's about creating the rigorous guardrails that make that speed count. This very page, and the tools showcased here, were built using the artifacts generated by the Product Trio framework.
